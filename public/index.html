About the study

Purpose

This study examines how social norms influence individual decision-making. Specifically, we look at how knowing what others think you ought to do affects the choices you make when working in a team.

What happens

You will be paired with another participant and asked to choose how much effort to contribute to a shared team project. Your earnings depend on both your effort and your partner’s. You will also receive ₹200 as a participation fee, plus additional earnings based on your decisions.

Who can join

The study is open to students aged 18–35 who are currently enrolled in a college or university. Sessions are held on the Ashoka University campus and take approximately 45–60 minutes.
