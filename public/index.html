Study background

About the study

Purpose

CSBC studies how social context and environment shape the decisions people make. This study examines decision-making and behavioural patterns among young adults in India.

What happens

Eligible participants attend one session on the Ashoka University campus and complete a short guided activity with the CSBC research team. Sessions typically last 45–60 minutes.

Who can join

The study is open to students aged 18–26 who are currently enrolled in a college or university. Participants will be screened for eligibility before a slot is confirmed.
