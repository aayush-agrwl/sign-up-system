Study background

About the study

Purpose

  • This study attempts to understand how people behave in team settings.
  • By participating, you can earn Rs. 100 to Rs. 500 based on your decisions.
  • Compensation is provided in the form of Amazon Gift Cards.

Who can join

  • Students aged 18–35 currently enrolled in any college or university.
  • The study is conducted in-person at the CSBC office in Vasant Vihar — only students based in Delhi/NCR who can conveniently travel to Vasant Vihar are invited. Travel costs will not be compensated.
  • Participants must carry a valid student ID card.
  • The study takes approximately 10–15 minutes to complete.
